# Approvals — Overview

The Approvals module is where authorised staff review submitted forms and make approve or reject decisions. SWIMS supports up to **five sequential approval levels**, each corresponding to a role in the decision-making hierarchy.

## How Approvals Work

When a form is submitted, it enters the approval queue at **Level 1**. The Level 1 approver reviews it and either:

- **Approves** → the form moves to Level 2 (if configured), or is marked fully approved (if L1 is the final level).
- **Rejects** → the form is immediately marked Rejected and the workflow ends.

This continues through each active level until the form is fully approved or rejected.

## Approval Levels & Roles

| Level | Typical Role |
|-------|-------------|
| **L1** | Social Worker |
| **L2** | Coordinator |
| **L3** | Director |
| **L4** | Permanent Secretary |
| **L5** | Minister |

Not all levels are active for every form — the programme manager configures which levels apply to each form. You will only see submissions at the levels your account has permission for.

## Your Approvals Dashboard

Navigate to **Approvals** in the sidebar to open your approvals dashboard. You will see:

- A list of forms with pending submissions at your level(s)
- The count of pending submissions per form
- Quick links to open each pending submission for review

> [!NOTE]
> You will only see submissions at the approval levels assigned to your account. If a submission is at Level 3 but you only have L1 and L2 permissions, it will not appear in your queue.

## Pending Count Guard

Some forms have a maximum pending count configured per level. If the queue at your level reaches this limit, new submissions for that form will be temporarily blocked from entering your queue. This prevents an unmanageable backlog. If you see a "Queue full" message on a form submission, it means the approver at the next level needs to process items before new ones can enter.
