# Reviewing & Approving

## Finding Submissions to Review

1. Navigate to **Approvals** in the sidebar.
2. Your dashboard shows all forms with pending submissions at your approval level(s).
3. The number next to each form name indicates how many submissions are waiting.
4. Click on a form name to see the list of pending submissions.
5. Click a submission to open the **Approval View**.

## The Approval View

The Approval View shows:

- **The complete form submission** — all field values as entered by the submitter, in read-only format
- **Submission details** — who submitted it, when, and for which beneficiary
- **Approval history** — previous approval actions (if any earlier levels have already approved)
- **Your action panel** — the Approve and Reject buttons, plus a notes field

## Making a Decision

### Approve

1. Review the submission thoroughly.
2. Optionally enter **notes** in the notes field (e.g. conditions, observations).
3. Click **Approve**.
4. Confirm the action in the prompt.

The submission will:
- Move to the next approval level (if further levels are configured), **or**
- Be marked **Fully Approved** (if this is the final level)

If this was the form's final approval level and the form is linked to a Case as the Primary Application, the case status will automatically update to **Active**.

### Reject

1. Enter **rejection notes** explaining the reason — this is good practice and may be required depending on your organisation's policy.
2. Click **Reject**.
3. Confirm the action in the prompt.

The submission is immediately marked **Rejected**. The workflow ends — no further approval levels are triggered. If this submission was the Primary Application for a Case, the case status will update to **Closed**.

## Viewing Your Decision History

You can view past approval decisions you have made by checking the **Approval History** section on any form submission's detail view. Each decision records:

- The approver's name
- The level at which the decision was made
- The decision (Approved / Rejected)
- The timestamp
- Any notes entered

## Common Scenarios

### Submission needs correction

SWIMS v1 does not have a "Return for Correction" workflow — you must either approve or reject. If a submission needs minor corrections, coordinate with the submitter directly (via Messenger or by phone) and reject with clear notes, so they can resubmit.

### You approved by mistake

Contact your SWIMS administrator. There is no self-service undo for approval decisions in v1.

### A submission has been waiting too long

Check the approval history to see which level it is currently at. If it is waiting at a level below yours, the relevant approver needs to act. Use the Messenger or other channels to follow up.
