# Filling In a Form

## Starting a New Submission

1. Navigate to **Programmes** in the sidebar.
2. Find the programme and form you need.
3. Click **Fill In** (or **New Submission**).
4. The form opens ready for data entry.

## Completing the Form

Work through the form fields from top to bottom:

- **Text fields** — type directly into the box.
- **Dropdown / Select** — click the field and choose from the list.
- **Date fields** — click the calendar icon to pick a date, or type in `DD/MM/YYYY` format.
- **Radio buttons** — click one option to select it.
- **Checkboxes** — click to tick or untick.
- **File upload fields** — click **Browse** or **Choose File**, locate the file on your computer, and confirm. Supported formats are shown next to the field.
- **Text areas** — for longer free-text entries.

### Required Fields

Fields marked with a red asterisk `*` are **required**. The form cannot be submitted until all required fields have a value.

### Conditional Fields

Some fields only appear when certain other fields are filled in or a specific option is selected. For example, a field asking for "Disability details" might only appear if you selected "Yes" to "Does the applicant have a disability?". This is normal — the form is adapting to your answers automatically.

## Saving Progress

SWIMS does not currently auto-save form drafts. **Complete and submit the form in one session** where possible. If you must leave, note which fields you have filled in — you will need to start a new submission when you return.

> [!TIP]
> For complex forms, prepare the information you need beforehand (e.g. beneficiary ID number, household income details, supporting documents) so you can complete it in one go.

## Submitting the Form

Once all required fields are complete:

1. Review your entries — scroll through the form to check everything looks correct.
2. Click the **Submit** button at the bottom of the form.
3. A confirmation screen will appear showing the submission was received.
4. The submission enters the **approval queue** at Level 1 automatically.

After submitting, you cannot edit the form unless an approver returns it to you.

## Viewing Your Submission

After submitting, you can view the form at any time by navigating to the relevant programme and clicking on your submission in the list. You will be able to see all the values you entered and the current approval status.

## Linking a Form to a Case

Submitted forms can be linked to an existing Case by a user with case management permissions. See [Managing a Case](../cases/managing.md) for how this works.
