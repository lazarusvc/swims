# Browsing Programmes

## The Programme Dashboard

Navigate to **Programmes** in the sidebar to open the Programme Dashboard. Here you will see all active social welfare programmes, each shown as a card or group containing its associated forms.

Click a programme name or the **expand** button to see the forms within it.

## Finding a Form

Within a programme, forms are listed with their name and a brief description. You can:

- Click a form name to **view** its details and any existing submissions
- Click **Fill In** or **New Submission** to start filling in a fresh copy of the form
- Click **Preview** to see the form layout without submitting

## Form Status Indicators

When viewing a list of submissions for a form, each row shows a status:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Draft** | Started but not yet submitted |
| **Submitted / Pending** | Submitted and waiting at an approval level |
| **Approved** | All required approval levels have approved |
| **Rejected** | Rejected at one of the approval levels |
| **Complete** | Fully processed and closed |

## Filtering and Searching

On the programme dashboard and within form submission lists, use the filter controls at the top of the page to narrow results by:

- Status
- Date submitted
- Submitted by
- Assigned case

## Viewing a Submitted Form

Click any submission in the list to open its **detail view**. This shows:

- All field values as entered
- The current approval status and which level it is at
- The history of approval actions taken
- Any linked case

## Accessing the Approval View

If you are an approver and a submission is waiting at your level, you can access the approval action screen directly from the form detail view. See [Reviewing & Approving](../approvals/reviewing.md).
