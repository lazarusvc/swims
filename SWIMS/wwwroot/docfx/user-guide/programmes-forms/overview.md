# Programmes & Forms — Overview

The **Programmes** section is where social welfare programmes are organised and their associated forms are accessed. Every form in SWIMS belongs to a programme — for example, "Public Assistance" or "Elderly Assistance (65+)".

## What Is a Programme?

A programme is a social welfare initiative run by the Ministry. Each programme has one or more **forms** — structured documents used to capture application details, assessments, referrals, or other information from or about a beneficiary.

## What Is a Form?

A form in SWIMS is an electronic document with a set of fields to fill in. Forms can include text fields, dropdowns, date pickers, checkboxes, and file upload fields. Some fields may show or hide automatically depending on your answers (conditional logic).

Forms go through an **approval workflow** — after a form is submitted, one or more approvers must review and approve it before it is considered complete.

## Who Can Do What

| Action | Required Permission |
|--------|-------------------|
| Browse programmes and view forms | `Programs.View` |
| Fill in and submit a form | `Forms.Submit` |
| Build or edit form templates | `Forms.Builder` |
| Create/manage form definitions | `Forms.Manage` |

Contact your administrator if you need access to a section you cannot see.
