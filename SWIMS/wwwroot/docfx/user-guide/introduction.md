# Welcome to SWIMS

**SWIMS** (Social Welfare Information Management System) is the digital platform used by the Government of the Commonwealth of Dominica to manage social welfare programmes. It gives staff a central place to register beneficiaries, process applications, manage cases, run approvals, and generate reports.

## Who This Guide Is For

This guide is written for **day-to-day staff users** of SWIMS — social workers, coordinators, directors, and administrative officers. It covers everything you need to use the system confidently, without requiring any technical knowledge.

If you are a developer or system administrator maintaining the SWIMS codebase, refer to the [Developer Guide](../dev/introduction.md) instead.

## What You Can Do in SWIMS

| Module | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Programmes & Forms** | Browse social welfare programmes, fill in application and assessment forms |
| **Cases** | Create and manage beneficiary cases through their full lifecycle |
| **Approvals** | Review and approve/reject submitted forms through up to five approval levels |
| **Beneficiaries** | Register and maintain the registry of individuals receiving assistance |
| **Messenger** | Communicate with colleagues in real time within SWIMS |
| **Notifications** | Receive alerts for new messages, approval actions, and system events |
| **Reports** | Run operational reports via the integrated SSRS reporting service |
| **Administration** | Manage users, roles, system settings, and monitoring (Admin only) |

## Getting Help

If you encounter an issue not covered in this guide:

1. Check the [Quick Reference](quick-reference/common-tasks.md) section for common task walkthroughs.
2. Contact your system administrator.
3. For technical issues, refer to the support contact details configured in your SWIMS instance under **System Settings**.

> [!NOTE]
> SWIMS is a confidential Government of Dominica system. Do not share your login credentials. Always log out when leaving your workstation.
