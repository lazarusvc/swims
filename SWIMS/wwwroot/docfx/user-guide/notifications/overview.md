# Notifications — Overview

SWIMS notifies you of important events through multiple channels so you can stay on top of activity without constantly refreshing the application.

## Notification Channels

| Channel | What it is |
|---------|-----------|
| **In-App Bell** | The notification bell (🔔) in the top bar — shows a badge and dropdown list |
| **Email** | An email sent to your registered address |
| **Daily Digest** | A single daily summary email (sent at 8:00 AM) grouping all your unread notifications |
| **Push Notification** | A native browser/device notification, even when SWIMS is not open (requires setup) |

## Types of Notifications

Notifications are triggered by events in SWIMS. Common types include:

| Event | Who gets notified |
|-------|-----------------|
| New chat message | The recipient of the message |
| Form submitted for approval | Approvers at the relevant level |
| Form approved / rejected | The original submitter |
| Case status changed | Assigned case worker |
| System events | Configured by administrator |

## The Notification Bell

Click the 🔔 **bell icon** in the top bar to open your notification panel:

- **Unread count** — shown as a blue badge on the bell
- **Notification list** — click any notification to go to the relevant record
- **Mark as read** — click a notification or use "Mark all as read" at the bottom

## Managing Your Preferences

See [Managing Notification Preferences](preferences.md) to control which channels are active for each notification type.
