# Navigating SWIMS

## The Layout

After logging in, you will see the main SWIMS interface made up of three areas:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Top Bar  — Search · Notifications Bell · Your Account  │
├───────────┬─────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│           │                                             │
│  Sidebar  │            Main Content Area               │
│  (left)   │                                             │
│           │                                             │
└───────────┴─────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## The Sidebar

The left sidebar is your main navigation. It shows only the sections **you have permission to access** — if a section does not appear, your account does not have access to it. Contact your administrator if you believe you are missing access.

Common sidebar sections:

| Section | What you'll find there |
|---------|----------------------|
| **Home / Dashboard** | Summary view and quick links |
| **Programmes** | Browse social welfare programmes and their forms |
| **Cases** | Your assigned cases and the full case list |
| **Approvals** | Forms waiting for your approval action |
| **Beneficiaries** | The beneficiary/client registry |
| **Reports** | SSRS-powered operational reports |
| **Messenger** | Real-time chat with colleagues |
| **Admin** | System administration (Admin users only) |

## The Top Bar

The top bar contains:

- **🔔 Notifications bell** — click to see your recent notifications. Unread notifications show a badge count.
- **Your name / avatar** — click to access your account settings, profile, and the Sign Out option.

## Notifications Bell

Click the bell icon to open your notification panel. From here you can:

- View recent notifications
- Click a notification to navigate to the relevant record
- Mark individual notifications as read
- Mark all as read

A blue badge on the bell shows how many unread notifications you have.

## Breadcrumbs

Most pages show a breadcrumb trail below the top bar, e.g.:

`Cases > Case Detail > CASE-2025-00042`

Click any part of the breadcrumb to navigate back up.

## Search

The search bar in the top area (where available) lets you search across beneficiaries, cases, and forms. Type at least 3 characters to trigger results.
