# Your Account

Your account settings are accessible by clicking your **name or avatar** in the top-right corner, then selecting **Manage Account** or navigating to **Account Settings**.

## Profile

Your profile page shows your:

- Display name
- Email address
- Account type (Local or Active Directory)
- Roles assigned to your account

Profile details for **Active Directory accounts** are synced from the directory and cannot be changed within SWIMS.

## Changing Your Password

*Available for local SWIMS accounts only. AD accounts must use your organisation's password policy.*

1. Go to **Account → Change Password**.
2. Enter your **current password**.
3. Enter and confirm your **new password**.
4. Click **Update Password**.

**Password requirements:**
- Minimum 6 characters
- Must include at least one uppercase letter
- Must include at least one digit

## Changing Your Email Address

*Available for local SWIMS accounts only.*

1. Go to **Account → Change Email**.
2. Enter your new email address.
3. Click **Send Verification Email**.
4. Check your inbox and click the confirmation link.
5. Your email is updated once confirmed.

## Two-Factor Authentication

See [Two-Factor Authentication](two-factor.md) for full setup instructions.

## Downloading Your Personal Data

You can download a copy of the personal data SWIMS holds about your account:

1. Go to **Account → Personal Data**.
2. Click **Download**.
3. A JSON file containing your account data will be downloaded.

## Deleting Your Account

Account deletion must be handled by your SWIMS administrator. Contact them directly if required.

## External Logins

The **External Logins** section shows whether your account is linked to any external authentication providers (e.g. Active Directory). For AD-linked accounts this section confirms the link is active.
