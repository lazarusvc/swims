# Starting a Conversation

## Starting a New Conversation

1. Navigate to **Messenger** in the sidebar.
2. Click **New Conversation** (or the compose/pencil icon).
3. Search for the colleague you want to message by typing their name.
4. Select them from the results.
5. Click **Start Conversation** (or just type your first message and send).

The conversation opens immediately.

## Sending a Message

1. Click on a conversation in your list to open it.
2. Type your message in the text box at the bottom.
3. Press **Enter** or click the **Send** button.

Messages are delivered in real time — if the other person has SWIMS open, they will see your message immediately.

## Receiving Messages

When you receive a new message:

- The **Notifications bell** in the top bar will show a badge
- A **desktop push notification** will appear (if you have enabled push notifications)
- An **email** may be sent (depending on your notification preferences)
- The conversation in your Messenger list will move to the top with an unread count

## Viewing a User's Profile

Click a user's name or avatar within a conversation to view their **profile card**, which shows their name, role, and contact details within SWIMS.

## Message History

Scroll up in any conversation to load older messages. The full history of the conversation is preserved.

## Notifications for Messages

You can control how you are notified about new messages in your [Notification Preferences](../notifications/preferences.md). Options include in-app bell, email, and push notification.
